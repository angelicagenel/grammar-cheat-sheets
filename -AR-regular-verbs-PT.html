💡 What are Regular -AR Verbs?

• Regular -AR verbs form the largest group in Spanish and follow predictable patterns.

• Infinitives are unconjugated verbs (trabajar, estudiar, caminar) that don't express who is doing the action.

🎯 When to Use Present Tense

✨ Current Truth

What is true now

Soy estudiante (I am a student)

🔄 Regular Actions

What happens regularly

Estudio todos los días (I study daily)

⏰ Right Now

What is happening now

Camino a casa (I'm walking home)

💡 Common Examples

Yo trabajo en una oficina
I work in an office
¿Tú estudias español?
Do you study Spanish?
Ella camina al parque
She walks to the park
Nosotros hablamos inglés
We speak English
Ellos viajan mucho
They travel a lot
¿Ustedes practican deportes?
Do you all practice sports?

⚠️ Important Note

The verb "gustar" (to like) does NOT follow these regular -AR patterns.
